ABOUT THE COMPANY

Headquartered in Beijing, PingCAP is a thriving company that develops TiDB, an open source distributed Hybrid Transactional/Analytical Processing (HTAP) database. The majority of PingCAP’s senior engineers and architects worked for famous internet giants, such as Twitter.com, JD.com, Baidu.com, Qihoo 360, Sogou.com, Wandou Labs, etc.
